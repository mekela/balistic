HESCO products have been protecting brave servicemen and women in the military for more than 20 years, and with our cutting edge ballistic inserts we bring that protection and confidence to the heroes of law enforcement.

EXCEEDING STANDARDS

Our products are engineered to exceed rigorous NIJ standards.
Tests are conducted at world class third party laboratories under the most stringent protocols.

Tests include:

  • Kinetic impact (ballistic, blast, fragment, stab)
  • Operational durability (drop impact, vibration)
  • Temperature extremes and shock
  • UV and weather
  • Chemical, fungus and fluid resistance
  • Altitude
  • X-ray inspection
  • Buoyancy
  • Human factors (comfort and ergonomics)
  • Complete quality inspection

DEFINE THE APPROPRIATE THREAT PROFILE

FITTED FOR PURPOSE ARMOR SIZE GUIDE

HESCO BODY ARMOR is available in a number of shapes and sizes as well as various curvatures, weights and thicknesses. A properly fitted torso plate should not interfere with the comfortable movement of the arms and shoulders. If a plate is too large, it can impede mobility and result in difficulty holding, aiming or discharging a weapon which can result in fatigue and inaccuracy.
